      Click cells to build a beat — drag to paint a row, right-click a cell to
      cycle its accent (ghost → normal → accent). Space plays/stops.
      bars sets the pattern length; M mutes, S solos, and the two
      sliders set a track's volume and stereo pan. Drop an audio file on a track (or
      hit 📁) to play your own sample instead of the synth — it's stored only
      in your browser. Everything you
      hear is the same procedural synth the audx CLI uses — and
      copy as audx DSL gives you a pattern you can paste straight into
      audx. share link puts the whole groove in a URL to send
      to anyone; download wav renders it to a file. Your work autosaves in
      this browser. connect MIDI / Push 2 (Chrome/Edge) plays voices from a
      controller's pads and lights up a Push 2 — the same LED bytes the CLI sends.
      Nothing is uploaded; it all runs on your machine.
